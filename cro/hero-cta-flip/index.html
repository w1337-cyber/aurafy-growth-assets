Why the flip (in one paragraph)

Before — "See pricing"

Cold visitor lands. Primary CTA asks them to evaluate price.

They've not used the product yet. Pricing-grid evaluation triggers comparison-shopping ("is this worth $49?") before they've felt whether Aurafy fits. The strongest fact — no card required — is invisible. Best case they bounce to /pricing then leave; worst case they leave the hero entirely.

After — "Start free — no card"

Cold visitor lands. Primary CTA invites them to try, secondary keeps /pricing one click away.

Names the friction they'd ask about anyway ("will this charge me?"). Free tier is real and capped honestly in fine print so there's no surprise later. Price-shoppers still reach /pricing in one click. Pairs with CRO #001: warm/high-intent visitors get the founder-seat counter as a direct convert path.

What's needed to ship this

  • Phase 1 (ships today, ~10 min): swap hero primary button copy + URL (/pricing → app.aurafy.dev/signup); add the fine-print line.
  • Phase 2 (same deploy): add UTM parameters (utm_source=site&utm_medium=hero&utm_campaign=start-free) + data-track attributes so attribution is in place from day one.
  • Phase 3 (after 2 weeks of data): review free-signup rate AND free→paid rate. If free signups went up but conversion to paid dropped meaningfully, iterate copy ("Start free — full Pro features for 30 days, no card") or revert.
